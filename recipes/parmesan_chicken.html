Parmesan Chicken

Description

Simple ingredients and easy steps. Great for any occasion.

for the simple steps below and you'll have youself a great meal!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup of olive oil
  • 1 cup granted Parmesan cheese
  • 1 teaspoon celery salt
  • 1 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 8 (5 ounce) bonless, skinless chicken thights

Direction

  • 1) Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).Line

    a jelly roll pan with foil.

  • 2) Put olive oil in a shallow bowl. Mix together Parmesan

    cheese, celery salt, cayenne pepper, and garlic in another

    shallow bowl.

  • 3) Cut chicken thighs in half. Dip chicken pieces in olive

    oil so both sides are covered, then press one side in the

    Paermesan mixture until coated. Place on the prepared pan

    with the coating facing up.

  • 4) Bake in the preheated oven until chicken is no longer

    pink in the senter and the juices run clear, about 25 minutes.

    An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should

    read at least 165 degree F (74 degrees C).
