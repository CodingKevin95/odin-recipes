Ginger-Touch Oatmeal Peanut Butter Cookies

Description

A great dessert to finish off your great meal. Heck

just skip any meal and head straight towards the cookies!

They're definitely worth it.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup shortening
  • 1 cup peanut butter
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup chopped crystallized ginger

Direction

  • Prehead oven to 350 degree F (175 degree C).

  • In a medium bowl, cream together the shortening, butter,

    brown sugar and white sugar. Bear in the eggs, peanut butter

    and vanilla. Combine the flour, baking soda, salt and ground

    ginger, stir into the creamed mixture. Finally, stir in the

    rolled oats and candied ginger. Drop by rounded teadspoonful

    onto an unprepared cookie sheet.

  • Bake for 10 to 12 minutes in the preheated oven, until

    golden brown. Remove from the baking sheet to cool on wire rack.

    Store in an airtight container when cool.
